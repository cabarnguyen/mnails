Acrylic

Acrylic is the durable form of nail enhancements. Recommended for those with naturally weak and brittle nails. Popular, and one of the hardest forms of enhancement due to its durability and low maintenance.

  • Acrylic Full Set
  • Acrylic Fill Ins

Pink & white

Pink and White Acrylic also known as the “permanent french” or “two tone” is the most popular look among women. It’s classic, clean, shiny, and ready to go without having to dry, with no worries of polish wearing off. Its non-yellowing formula is done in a double process with white powder for the free edge and pink powder for nail bed.

  • Pink & White Full Set
  • Pink & White Fill Ins
  • Pink Fills Only
  • Pink & White Full Set (liquid)
  • Pink & White Fill Ins (liquid)
  • Pink Fills Only (liquid)

UV Gel

UV Gel is a great coating over natural or acrylic nails. The gel coating keeps your nails shiny and natural without polish. It is recommended for those who constantly work with their hands and don’t want to worry about their shine fading.

  • Gel Full Set (powder)
  • Gel Fill Ins (powder)
  • Gel Full Set (liquid)
  • Gel Fill Ins (liquid)
